Search for a Route

The Routes function retrieves the entries from the routes database table that match your departure, arrival, and day of the week criteria.

To find a flight

  1. Locate your preferred departing and arriving airports using the Country, Region, and Airport Code selection lists.
  2. Select the day of the week you want to fly, as follows:

    Enable the Any day option to see flights operating on any day of the week.

    Disable the Any day option to choose a specific day. You can select one or more days.

  3. Click Go.

    A list of flights matching your criteria is returned.

  4. (Optional) Click New Search to begin a new search.
